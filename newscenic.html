About

Daveed is your local Los Angeles beat maker with 5 years of creating independent tracks. He specializes in making trap beats featuring heavy 808's and up beat tempo. As he gains more experience in this field of work, Daveed has started experimenting with different styles and genres as it is apparent that the industries are aiming at the same sound, image, and messages. Daveed believes in diversity and hopes that he is not defined just by one type of sound. This website is dedicated to artist open to new sounds and ideas. Daveed also believes in stepping out of your comfort zone and looking the other direction that people tend to look towards. We are all unique individuals seeking certain things that defines us. Browse and define yourself!
